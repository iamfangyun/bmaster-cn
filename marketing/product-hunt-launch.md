## 描述（260字符电梯演讲）
Most mortgage calculators show you P&I and call it a day. This one shows the FULL picture: PMI (with auto-removal at 78% LTV), property tax by state, home insurance, HOA, closing costs, and a real amortization schedule. No signup. No email. No tracking.

## 完整描述

When I was house hunting, every calculator I found had the same problem: they showed the monthly P&I payment, but hid $300–500/month in PMI, property tax, and insurance. I'd think "I can afford $2,000/month" — then reality hit at closing.

So I built one that doesn't hide anything.

**What makes it different:**

🏠 **Full monthly breakdown** — P&I + PMI + Property Tax + Insurance + HOA, not just P&I

📊 **PMI auto-removal tracking** — Most calculators charge PMI forever. This one knows when you hit 78% LTV and shows you exactly when PMI drops off (and how much your payment decreases)

🗺️ **State property tax presets** — One click for CA, TX, NY, NJ, HI, etc. (rates range from 0.28% to 2.23%)

💰 **Closing costs breakdown** — Loan origination, title insurance, appraisal, escrow — see exactly what you'll pay at closing, not just "estimated fees"

📅 **Full amortization schedule** — See why the first 5 years feel like paying rent (most money goes to interest). Toggle on/off.

⚡ **Early payoff calculator** — Drag a slider to see how an extra $200/month saves $123,000 in interest and pays off 6 years early

🎨 **Clean, fast, no clutter** — No ads in the calculator itself, no popups, no "create an account". Loads instantly, works offline once loaded.

**Real example:**
- $400,000 home, 10% down, 7% APR, 30-year
- Other calculators say: "$2,395/month" ← that's just P&I
- This calculator says: "$3,068/month" (P&I + PMI + tax + insurance)
- And tells you PMI drops off at year 10, saving $156/month

**100% free. No signup. No email required. No data collected. Runs entirely in your browser.**

Try it: https://bmaster.cn/mortgage-calculator/
