产品名: Mortgage Calculator
URL: https://bmaster.cn/mortgage-calculator/
分类: Finance, Real Estate, Personal Finance, Calculators
定价: Free
平台: Web

一句话描述:
Honest mortgage calculator that shows your REAL monthly payment — including PMI, property tax, insurance, HOA, and closing costs that other calculators hide.

详细描述:
Most mortgage calculators only show principal and interest, hiding $300-700/month in additional costs. This one shows everything:

- Full monthly payment breakdown (P&I + PMI + Property Tax + Insurance + HOA)
- PMI auto-removal tracking at 78% LTV (know exactly when PMI drops off)
- State-specific property tax presets (0.28% Hawaii to 2.23% New Jersey)
- Closing costs breakdown (origination, title, appraisal, escrow)
- Full 30-year amortization schedule with PMI column
- Early payoff calculator (see how extra payments save interest)
- Visual chart showing principal vs interest over time

100% free, no signup, no email required, no data collected. Runs entirely in your browser.

主要功能（逗号分隔）:
PMI Calculator, Property Tax Calculator, Closing Cost Estimator, Amortization Schedule, Early Payoff Calculator, Mortgage Affordability
